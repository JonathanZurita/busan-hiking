Mt. Jangsan

Elevated at a height of 643 meters at it's peak, Jangsan is the tallest mountain in the City of Busan and features stunning 360 degree views of the beautiful building and attractions including Seomyeon, Busan's downtown district, the wonderful Gwangan Bridge, and gorgeous beaches of Haeundae.

Gwangan

This gorgeous beach overlooking the Gwangan Bridge, built in 1994, is a half-moon shape with fine sand. The adjacent street is lined with restaurants, coffee shops and nightclubs.

Igidae

Wonderful Oceanfront Forest hike with sections of boardwalk, trails and stream crossing due to diversion as a part of the trail was closed due to work going on. Beautiful views of the forest , cliffs and sea.
